# IntelliTalk

URL: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IntelliTalk

**IntelliTalk** is a [word processor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Word_processor) by [Synapse Adaptive](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Synapse_Adaptive&action=edit&redlink=1). It exists in the [IntelliTools](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=IntelliTools&action=edit&redlink=1) family of products, and is intended for students in [kindergarten](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kindergarten) through [eighth grade](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eighth_grade). IntelliTalk 3, the most recent version, has features that include:

- compatibility with [Macintosh](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Macintosh) and [Windows](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microsoft_Windows)
- voices that can read back typed letters, words, or sentences
- libraries of [graphics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer_graphics)
- integrated [word prediction](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Word_prediction)
- spell checker
- ability to easily change colours, fonts, and sizes
- worksheet creation
- customizable toolbars
- student portfolio management

The software, intended to aid students with [disabilities](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Disability) is built on the philosophy of [universal access](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Universal_access).[[1]](https://archive.is/20130203203337/http://www.synapse-ada.com/intellitools/new/IntelliTalk_3.htm)
